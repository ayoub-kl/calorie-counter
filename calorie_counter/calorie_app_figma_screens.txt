
============================================================
SCREEN 1 — Splash Screen
============================================================
Title:
Splash Screen

Description:
A clean startup screen shown while the app loads.

The screen displays the app logo centered on the screen with a subtle gradient background inspired by health and nutrition themes (fresh green, soft neutral tones).
Below the logo there is a small loading indicator.

Purpose:
- initialize app
- load preferences
- prepare navigation

Elements:
- centered app logo
- subtle animated loader
- soft gradient background
